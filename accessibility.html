Accessibility Statement

The site is designed to support readable, keyboard-friendly and privacy-respecting access across devices.

Accessibility approach

This portfolio aims to remain usable with keyboard navigation, screen readers, zoom, mobile devices and reduced-motion preferences. Balanced content protection is enabled to discourage casual copying, text selection, dragging and printing. Interactive links, buttons and form controls remain available. Anyone who needs an accessible copy of specific information may request it by email.

Features included

  • Semantic headings and landmarks.
  • Skip-to-content link.
  • Visible keyboard focus and accessible buttons.
  • Alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Responsive layouts for mobile, tablet and desktop.
  • Reduced-motion support based on device preferences.
  • Privacy choices available without forcing optional analytics.

Feedback

If you experience an accessibility problem, contact priyanthafly@gmail.com and describe the page, device and issue. Reasonable efforts will be made to provide the information in an accessible alternative.
