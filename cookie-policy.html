Cookie & Analytics Policy

Optional analytics are privacy-controlled and do not load until the visitor agrees.

1. Necessary browser storage

The site may use local browser storage for the selected colour theme and the visitor's analytics-consent choice. This supports requested functionality and privacy preferences. It is not used for advertising.

Typical local-storage keys

  • ht-theme - remembers light or dark appearance.
  • ht-analytics-consent - remembers whether optional analytics were accepted or rejected.

2. Optional analytics

Google Analytics and/or Cloudflare Web Analytics can be configured by the site owner. They do not load before the visitor accepts optional analytics. If no analytics identifiers are configured, no optional analytics scripts are loaded.

When configured and accepted, analytics may measure visits, devices, general locations, traffic sources and interactions such as document or contact-link clicks. Advertising personalisation and Google Signals are disabled in the site configuration.

3. Accept, reject or withdraw

The consent banner provides equally accessible options to accept, reject or manage optional analytics. Choices can later be changed using the Privacy settings link in the footer. Rejecting optional analytics does not prevent access to the portfolio.

4. Third-party links

Opening LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, WhatsApp, Google Maps or credential-verification pages leaves this website. Those services may use cookies or similar technologies under their own policies.

5. Service worker and cache

The site may use a service worker and browser cache to improve loading and offline reliability. Cached public files do not create a recruitment profile and are not used for behavioural advertising.
