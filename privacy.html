Privacy Notice

This notice explains how personal data may be handled when you visit or contact this personal professional portfolio.

1. Who is responsible for the data?

The controller of personal data submitted directly through professional contact is Priyantha Harispaththuwa Tennege, Siedlce, Poland. Contact: priyanthafly@gmail.com.

This is a personal professional portfolio. It is not an official recruitment application portal and is not presented as a registered recruitment agency.

2. What data may be processed?

The website itself does not contain an application form. Personal data may be received when a visitor chooses to contact the portfolio owner by email, phone, WhatsApp or LinkedIn. This may include a name, contact details, message content and any information voluntarily supplied.

Necessary browser storage may remember colour-theme and privacy choices. Optional analytics may process technical and usage information only after consent and only if analytics IDs are configured.

3. Purposes and legal bases

  • Responding to professional enquiries and maintaining professional relationships - legitimate interests under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Taking steps requested before a possible professional or business arrangement - Article 6(1)(b) GDPR where applicable.
  • Optional website analytics - consent under Article 6(1)(a) GDPR.
  • Establishing, exercising or defending legal claims - legitimate interests or legal obligations where applicable.

4. Hosting and recipients

The site is hosted through GitHub Pages, a service operated by GitHub/Microsoft. Hosting providers may process technical service data such as IP address, device and request information for security and service operation under their own privacy terms.

When a visitor selects an external link, services such as Google Maps, LinkedIn, Meta platforms or WhatsApp may process information under their own privacy policies. The portfolio owner does not control those third-party services.

5. International transfers

Some service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where applicable, transfers are handled by those providers using legal transfer mechanisms described in their privacy documentation, such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.

6. Retention

Professional enquiries are normally retained only as long as necessary to reply and manage the relevant relationship, generally no longer than 12 months after the last meaningful contact unless a longer period is required by law, an ongoing relationship or the defence of legal claims. Analytics retention is controlled through the selected analytics provider and should be configured to the shortest practical period.

7. Your rights

Subject to the GDPR and applicable conditions, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability, or object to processing based on legitimate interests. Consent may be withdrawn at any time through the Privacy settings control. Withdrawal does not affect earlier lawful processing.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Polish supervisory authority, the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (Prezes Urzędu Ochrony Danych Osobowych - UODO).

8. No automated decision-making

This personal portfolio does not use personal data for automated decisions or profiling that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

9. Candidate-document warning

Do not send passports, identity cards, residence permits, visas, medical records, criminal-record documents or other confidential candidate documents through this personal portfolio. Official applications and sensitive documents must be submitted only through authorised employer channels.

10. Public professional documents

The CV and general cover letter are voluntarily published by the portfolio owner for legitimate professional review. They contain professional contact information. They must not be republished, harvested, misused or used for unsolicited bulk marketing.

11. Updates

Last updated: 11 July 2026. This notice may be updated when the site, providers or legal requirements change.
