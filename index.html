ABOUT ME

End-to-end recruitment powered by people skills, digital reach and continuous learning.

I am a Sri Lankan recruitment professional based in Poland, supporting full-time end-to-end international recruitment across two Poland-based companies. My work connects workforce needs with suitable candidates through vacancy preparation, sourcing, screening, job matching, document checks, interview coordination, offer communication, mobility support, onboarding and post-placement follow-up.

I also create and adapt multilingual job posts, social media graphics and AI-assisted recruitment videos to explain vacancies clearly and reach candidates through LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube and WhatsApp.

My earlier experience in technology entrepreneurship, warehouse operations and quality control gives me a practical understanding of both people and workplace needs. I continuously develop knowledge in human resources, leadership, data, cybersecurity, communication and digital marketing to strengthen responsible recruitment practice.
